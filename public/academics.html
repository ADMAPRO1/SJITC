1. Department of Information and Communication Technology (ICT)

About the department

"Communication networks have evolved into critical platforms in the world of ICT, it is crucial in facilitating communication both inside organizations and on a personal level who have a comprehensive background in fundamental areas of information technology, as well as depth of understanding in a specific area of interest within the domain of networking and multimedia. This Department places a premium on students' knowledge bases in essential computer hardware, software, and information networks such as the internet."

Duration of the Program

For full time students, the duration is 3 Years

Job related information

Graduates of information technology can pursue a wide range of job prospects. Some careers that would benefit from a degree include Computer programmer, Application developer, Information security analyst, Information systems manager, IT consultant, Network administrator, Server administrator, Computer systems analyst, IT technical support officer, Multimedia programmer/specialist, Software tester, Systems analyst, Systems developer, IT project manager, Web designer, Web developer, IT trainer, and Secondary school teacher among many others.

2. Department of Civil Engineering

About the department

"Civil engineering plays a pivotal role in shaping the built environment, from infrastructure development to sustainable construction practices. It is a discipline that combines technical expertise, innovation, and problem-solving to create structures that meet societal needs while addressing environmental challenges. In this regard, the Department of Civil Engineering is committed to equipping students with a strong foundation in core areas of the discipline, including structural design, geotechnical engineering, transportation engineering, and water resources engineering. This Department emphasizes hands-on learning and theoretical understanding in key areas such as structural design, construction materials, geotechnical engineering, surveying, and sustainable building practices."

Duration of the Program

For full time students, the duration is 3 Years

Job related information

Graduates of Construction technology can pursue a wide range of job prospects. On completion of the qualification, learners will be able to gain access to job opportunities in the construction and building service sector both locally and internationally.
