Minimum Requirements

1. Advanced Diploma in Construction Technology

Eligible Candidate to this qualification should fulfill the following:

  • (a) Be a holder of Advanced Level Certificate (A’ Level) with two Principal Passes with a background in Mathematics.
  • (b) Be a holder of REQF Level V Certificate with a strong background in construction related Competencies or
  • (c) A Certificate of recognition of prior Learning at National Qualifications Framework equivalent to A Level or REQF Level 5 in Construction related fields.
  • (d) Any other equivalent Certificate obtained from other recognized institutions.

2. Advanced Diploma in Information Technology

Eligible Candidate to this qualification should fulfill the following

  • (a) Be a holder of Advanced Level Certificate (A’ Level) with two Principal Passes.
  • (b) Be a holder of REQF Level V Certificate with a strong background in software development or computer networking or
  • (c) A Certificate of Recognition of Prior Learning at National Qualifications Framework equivalent to A Level or REQF Level 5 in Information Technology Related Fields or
  • (d) Any other equivalent certificate obtained from other recognized institutions.
  • (e) Have a background in Basic Mathematics and Sciences including Physics, Basic Economics, and Computer Science related subjects.
